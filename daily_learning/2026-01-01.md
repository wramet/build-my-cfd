## 1. Theory: Core Equations & Physics

### 1.1 Conservation of Mass (Continuity Equation)

**สมการการอนุรักษ์มวล (Continuity Equation)**

$$
\frac{\partial \rho}{\partial t} + \nabla \cdot (\rho \mathbf{U}) = 0
$$

**คำอธิบายพจน์:**
- $\rho$ (rho) = **ความหนาแน่น** (density) [kg/m³]
- $\mathbf{U}$ = **สนามความเร็ว** (velocity field) [m/s]
- $\nabla \cdot$ = **ตัวดำเนินการไดเวอร์เจนซ์** (divergence operator)
- $t$ = **เวลา** (time) [s]

สำหรับการไหลแบบ **ไม่อัดตัว (incompressible flow)**: $\nabla \cdot \mathbf{U} = 0$

---

### 1.2 Conservation of Momentum (Navier-Stokes Equation)

**สมการการอนุรักษ์โมเมนตัม (Momentum Equation)**

$$
\frac{\partial (\rho \mathbf{U})}{\partial t} + \nabla \cdot (\rho \mathbf{U} \mathbf{U}) = -\nabla p + \nabla \cdot \boldsymbol{\tau} + \rho \mathbf{g}
$$

**คำอธิบายพจน์:**
- $p$ = **ความดัน** (pressure) [Pa]
- $\boldsymbol{\tau}$ (tau) = **เทนเซอร์ความเค้น** (stress tensor) [Pa]
- $\mathbf{g}$ = **ความเร่งเนื่องจากแรงโน้มถ่วง** (gravitational acceleration) [m/s²]
- $\nabla p$ = **แรงตามแนวความชันของความดัน** (pressure gradient force)
- $\nabla \cdot \boldsymbol{\tau}$ = **แรงเฉือน** (viscous forces)

สำหรับ **นิวตันเหลว (Newtonian fluid)** แบบไม่อัดตัว:

$$
\frac{\partial \mathbf{U}}{\partial t} + (\mathbf{U} \cdot \nabla) \mathbf{U} = -\frac{1}{\rho} \nabla p + \nu \nabla^2 \mathbf{U} + \mathbf{g}
$$

โดยที่ $\nu$ (nu) = **ความหนืดเชิงจลน์** (kinematic viscosity) [m²/s]

---

### 1.3 Conservation of Energy

**สมการการอนุรักษ์พลังงาน (Energy Equation)**

$$
\frac{\partial (\rho e)}{\partial t} + \nabla \cdot (\rho e \mathbf{U}) = -\nabla \cdot \mathbf{q} + \boldsymbol{\tau} : \nabla \mathbf{U} + S
$$

**คำอธิบายพจน์:**
- $e$ = **พลังงานภายในต่อหน่วยมวล** (specific internal energy) [J/kg]
- $\mathbf{q}$ = **ฟลักซ์ความร้อน** (heat flux) [W/m²]
- $\boldsymbol{\tau} : \nabla \mathbf{U}$ = **การกระจายพลังงานเนื่องจากความเค้น** (viscous dissipation)
- $S$ = **แหล่งกำเนิดพลังงาน** (energy source) [W/m³]

---

### 1.4 Equation of State

**สมการสถานะ (Equation of State)**

สำหรับ **แก๊สอุดมคติ (Ideal Gas)**:

$$
p = \rho R T
$$

**คำอธิบายพจน์:**
- $R$ = **ค่าคงที่ของแก๊ส** (specific gas constant) [J/(kg·K)]
- $T$ = **อุณหภูมิ** (temperature) [K]

สำหรับ **ของไหลไม่อัดตัว (Incompressible Fluid)**:

$$
\rho = \text{constant}
$$

---

### 1.5 Transport Equations (General Form)

**สมการเชิงการขนส่งทั่วไป (General Transport Equation)**

$$
\frac{\partial (\rho \phi)}{\partial t} + \nabla \cdot (\rho \phi \mathbf{U}) = \nabla \cdot (\Gamma \nabla \phi) + S_\phi
$$

**คำอธิบายพจน์:**
- $\phi$ (phi) = **ปริมาณที่เคลื่อนที่** (transported quantity) - สามารถเป็นความเร็ว, อุณหภูมิ, ความเข้มข้น ฯลฯ
- $\Gamma$ (Gamma) = **สัมประสิทธิ์การแพร่** (diffusion coefficient)
- $S_\phi$ = **เทอมแหล่งกำเนิด** (source term)

**การตีความ:**
- $\frac{\partial (\rho \phi)}{\partial t}$ = **เทอมเปลี่ยนแปลงตามเวลา** (transient term)
- $\nabla \cdot (\rho \phi \mathbf{U})$ = **เทอมการพาความ** (convection term)
- $\nabla \cdot (\Gamma \nabla \phi)$ = **เทอมการแพร่** (diffusion term)
- $S_\phi$ = **เทอมแหล่งกำเนิด/ตัวรับ** (source/sink term)

## 2. OpenFOAM Class Hierarchy & Implementation

### 2.1 Core Field Classes

OpenFOAM represents governing equations through **field classes** that hold data at mesh points/cells.

```
GeometricField (Templated abstract base)
│
├── volScalarField  (Cell-centered scalar values)
├── volVectorField  (Cell-centered vector values)
├── volTensorField  (Cell-centered tensor values)
├── surfaceScalarField  (Face-centered scalar values)
└── surfaceVectorField  (Face-centered vector values)
```

**Key Files:**
- `$FOAM_SRC/finiteVolume/fields/volFields/volFields.H`
- `$FOAM_SRC/finiteVolume/fields/surfaceFields/surfaceFields.H`
- `$FOAM_SRC/finiteVolume/fields/geometricFields/geometricFields.H`

---

### 2.2 Matrix & Solver Classes

The discretized equations form linear systems $Ax = b$ solved through:

```
lduMatrix (Lower-Diagonal-Upper matrix)
│
├── fvMatrix (Finite Volume matrix)
│   ├── Solver
│   │   ├── GAMG (Geometric-Algebraic Multi-Grid)
│   │   ├── PCG (Preconditioned Conjugate Gradient)
│   │   ├── PBiCGStab (Preconditioned Bi-Conjugate Gradient Stabilized)
│   │   └── smoothSolver
│   └── Solution (Holds solution vector)
│
└── lduAddressing (Matrix connectivity)
```

**Key Files:**
- `$FOAM_SRC/finiteVolume/fields/fvMatrices/fvMatrix/fvMatrix.H`
- `$FOAM_SRC/matrices/lduMatrix/lduMatrix.H`
- `$FOAM_SRC/matrices/lduMatrix/solvers/`
- `$FOAM_SRC/finiteVolume/lnInclude/fvSolution.H`

---

### 2.3 Discretization Schemes

Spatial and temporal discretization schemes:

```
discretisationScheme (Abstract base)
│
├── convectionScheme
│   ├── Gauss (Gauss theorem-based)
│   │   ├── linear (Central differencing)
│   │   ├── linearUpwind (Upwind-biased)
│   │   ├── QUICK (Quadratic Upwind Interpolation)
│   │   └── limitedLinear (TVD schemes)
│   └── upwind (First-order upwind)
│
├── divergenceScheme
├── laplacianScheme
│   ├── Gauss (Gauss theorem)
│   └── uncorrected (Basic orthogonal)
│
└── ddtScheme (Time derivative)
    ├── Euler (First-order implicit)
    ├── backward (Second-order implicit)
    └── CrankNicolson (Second-order trapezoidal)
```

**Key Files:**
- `$FOAM_SRC/finiteVolume/finiteVolume/ddtSchemes/`
- `$FOAM_SRC/finiteVolume/finiteVolume/interpolation/interpolationSchemes/`
- `$FOAM_SRC/finiteVolume/finiteVolume/convectionSchemes/`
- `$FOAM_SRC/finiteVolume/finiteVolume/lnInclude/fvSchemes.H`

---

### 2.4 Boundary Condition Classes

Boundary conditions enforce physical constraints at domain boundaries:

```
fvPatchField (Base boundary condition)
│
├── fixedValue (Dirichlet BC)
├── zeroGradient (Neumann BC: ∂φ/∂n = 0)
├── fixedGradient (Neumann BC: ∂φ/∂n = constant)
│
├── mixedRobin (Robin BC: aφ + b(∂φ/∂n) = c)
│
├── inletOutlet (Switches between inlet/outlet)
├── outletInlet (Switches between outlet/inlet)
│
├── cyclic (Periodic BC)
├── processorCyclic (MPI parallel periodic)
│
├── wall (Wall functions for turbulence)
├── symmetryPlane (Symmetry condition)
└── empty (2D/1D reduction)
```

**Key Files:**
- `$FOAM_SRC/finiteVolume/fields/fvPatchFields/`
- `$FOAM_SRC/finiteVolume/fields/fvPatchFields/basic/`
- `$FOAM_SRC/finiteVolume/fields/fvPatchFields/constraint/`
- `$FOAM_SRC/finiteVolume/fields/fvPatchFields/derived/`

---

### 2.5 Mesh & Topology Classes

The computational domain representation:

```
polyMesh (Unstructured polyhedral mesh)
│
├── primitiveMesh (Basic mesh connectivity)
│   ├── points (Vertex coordinates)
│   ├── faces (Face-point lists)
│   └── cells (Cell-face lists)
│
├── fvMesh (Finite Volume mesh)
│   ├── Sf (Surface area vectors)
│   ├── magSf (Face area magnitudes)
│   ├── C (Cell centers)
│   ├── Cf (Face centers)
│   └── delta (Cell-face distance vectors)
│
└── lduMesh (Matrix addressing mesh)
    └── lduAddressing (Owner-neighbor lists)
```

**Key Files:**
- `$FOAM_SRC/meshes/polyMesh/polyMesh.H`
- `$FOAM_SRC/finiteVolume/fvMesh/fvMesh.H`
- `$FOAM_SRC/meshes/meshShapes/`
- `$FOAM_SRC/meshes/primitiveMesh/`

---

### 2.6 Physics Model Classes

Turbulence and thermophysical models:

```
compressible::turbulenceModel
│
├── RASModel (Reynolds-Averaged Simulation)
│   ├── kEpsilon (k-ε model)
│   ├── kOmegaSST (k-ω SST model)
│   ├── SpalartAllmaras (SA model)
│   └── LRR (Launder-Reece-Rodi)
│
├── LESModel (Large Eddy Simulation)
│   ├── Smagorinsky
│   ├── dynamicKEqn
│   └── WALE
│
└── DESModel (Detached Eddy Simulation)

basicThermo
│
├── heThermo (Enthalpy-based)
├── eThermo (Internal energy-based)
│
└── specieThermo
    ├── perfectGas (Ideal gas)
    └── icoPolynomial (Incompressible liquid)
```

**Key Files:**
- `$FOAM_SRC/turbulenceModels/`
- `$FOAM_SRC/thermophysicalModels/`
- `$FOAM_SRC/turbulenceModels/compressible/RAS/`
- `$FOAM_SRC/turbulenceModels/incompressible/LES/`

---

### 2.7 Time Integration Classes

Time-stepping control:

```
Time (Virtual time control)
│
├── runTime (Instantiation in solvers)
│   ├── timeName (Current time directory name)
│   ├── timeValue (Current simulation time)
│   ├── deltaT (Time step size)
│   └── endTime (Final simulation time)
│
└── Time::run() (Returns true while t < endTime)
```

**Key Files:**
- `$FOAM_SRC/OpenFOAM/db/Time/Time.H`
- `$FOAM_SRC/finiteVolume/lnInclude/fvCFD.H` (Includes runTime)

---

Configuration and data handling:

```
IOdictionary (Dictionary file I/O)

IOobject (File object management)

```

**Key Files:**
